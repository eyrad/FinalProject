Mabel K. Dzakuma, Assistant Director

Obtained her General Nursing and Midwifery education in England. She has 34 years of multispecialty nursing experience. Mabel is Board Certified in Oncology Nursing and has been practicing in the United States of America for the past 32 years. While practicing at the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, she was awarded Outstanding Nurse-of-the-Year for her unit, an award unanimously voted for by her peers. She currently practices as Charge Nurse at the Memorial Hermann the Woodlands Cancer Center where she continues to give outstanding compassionate care to patients and families; and coordinates care for patients. Mabel is committed to patient and family education and does so one-on-one and gives educational presentations to cancer support groups. She also shares her knowledge with staff on an ongoing basis through educational in-services. Her rich experience in health matters will go a long way in providing valuable advisory services to KAMMEY programs.

Mante K. Dzakuma, Advisory Services

Over six years of work experience and an extensive economics and land usage background to consistently establish himself as an invaluable resource to KAMMEY Solutions, Inc. Mante graduated from Harvard University in 2004 with a degree in Economics, where he was also a member of the Harvard Football team. As a senior strong safety, he received the prestigious Robert F. Kennedy award to honor his desire and determination on the field. Upon graduation, Mante went to work for Woodforest National Bank as a Credit Analyst. From there, he proceeded to advance his economic knowledge by becoming an Engagement Manager for The Concord Group. In this role, Mante advised on both commercial and residential real estate projects, providing research, analysis, and land use programming and positioning for leading developers and financial institutions across the country. Recently, Mante was an Account Manager at Meltwater News where he used his business acumen to help large and small businesses navigate the media space. Currently, he has returned to his economic roots within the real estate industry and provides on-demand consulting to prominent land developers. Mante’s intellect, hard working approach, and personable nature, propel him as a top KAMMEY Solutions, Inc. executive to empower individuals towards economic development.

Eyra A. Dzakuma, Consultant

Over five years of dedicated work experience within the corporate atmosphere and brings a valuable business and communications mindset to KAMMEY Solutions, Inc. After graduating from the University of Texas in 2005 with a concentration in Corporate Communications and Business Foundations, Eyra landed a highly competitive position as a Wienermobile Spokesperson for Oscar Mayer, driving the famed Oscar Mayer Wienermobile across the country while executing on various marketing, promotional, and goodwill events. From there, she went on to further her business relationships as a Sales Representative for Kraft Foods and as a Research Associate for Gerson Lehrman Group, where she leveraged vital communications with analysts at top institutional investment firms and professional service firms, to facilitate consulting engagements. Currently, Eyra is an Account Manager at Volusion, Inc., where she actively uses her communication skills to guide both large and medium companies along the path of having a successful e-commerce business. Eyra’s passion for connecting with individuals and impacting their lives is a welcomed asset for fulfilling the objectives of KAMMEY Solutions, Inc.
